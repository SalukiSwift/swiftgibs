# SwiftGibs v1.1

A quality-of-life update. Everything is client-side and server-compatible, so this build plays on the same servers as before.

## New

- **Selectable hit-marker sounds.** Pick the sound that plays when you land a hit (a stock option plus several alternates), preview it from the menu, and set its volume with a slider.
- **Killstreak counter.** A per-life streak counter on the HUD, with optional milestone dings. Team-kills do not count.
- **Career stats.** A stats page tracking your frags, deaths, K/D, accuracy, damage, and best killstreak across matches.
- **Readable chat.** Scroll back through the chat log with the mouse wheel while you type, or open a dedicated Chat History page.
- **Server browser toggle.** A "show all game modes" checkbox to see non-instagib servers when you want them.
- **Mute the rifle reload sound** if you prefer silence between shots.

## Changed

- **Reload cues are now off by default.** The reload countdown, fire-ready flash, and metronome are all opt-in. Turn on the ones you like in the new **Cues** tab.
- **New Cues settings tab.** Audio and visual feedback (reload cues, hit sounds, killstreak, teammate crosshair) now live in their own tab under Esc > options, alongside the SwiftGibs and Friends tabs.

## Download

Grab the build for your platform below and follow the README:

- **Windows:** `swiftgibs-win64.zip`
- **macOS (Apple Silicon):** `SwiftGibs-mac.zip`
- **Linux (x86-64):** `SwiftGibs-linux-x86_64.tar.gz`

Your settings and saved friends carry over from v1.0.
